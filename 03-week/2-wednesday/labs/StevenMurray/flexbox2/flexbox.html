7-December-1941 ~ 11-September-2001

Today is September 11, 2019

This was a dark day in our history.

I will never forget staring at a printout of a database

schema that I had on my office wall while I was listening to

the radio and heard that the first tower had collapsed.

Had I been younger I would have enlisted the next day.

I remember calling my dad and asking him if he was watching

the news, when he said he was watching something else, I told

him to turn Matlock off and turn on the news because we were

going to war.
