Hoth

As the film opens, we find Luke Skywalker astride his Tauntaun, patrolling the frozen dunes of the ice planet Hoth.

Wampa

Taking a break from his riding, Luke encounters a snow monster called a Wampa.

Han Solo

When Luke fails to return to base on schedule, Han Solo ventures into the cold on his own Tauntaun to find his missing friend.

The Imperial Fleet

The Empire has tracked the Rebels to Hoth, launching an assault on the previously hidden Rebel base.

AT-AT

The Empire launches its attack on the Rebel base with All-Terrain Armored Transports.

Snowspeeder

The Rebels fight back in tiny fighters called snowspeeders.

The Battle of Hoth

The Empire routs the Rebels at Hoth, sending survivors scattering in all directions.

Jedi Master Yoda

Luke flees to the planet Dagobah, in search of the Jedi Master Yoda who had instructed Obi-Wan Kenobi.

Chase

Meahwhile, Han and Princess Leia flee aboard the Millennium Falcon, pursued by the Imperial fleet.

Asteroid Field

"They'd be crazy to follow us" - Han Solo as he pilots his ship into an asteroid field to elude his pursuers.

Han and Leia

With the Millennium Falcon parked inside a cave on one of the asteroids, Han steals a moment with Princess Leia.

Cloud City

Having evaded the Imperial fleet, Han takes his companion to Cloud City to find an old friend, Lando Calrissian, and get his ship repaired.

Betrayal

It's a double cross! Lando betrays Han and Leia to Darth Vader, who has arrived at Cloud City shortly before the Millennium Falcon.

Bounty Hunter, Boba Fett

Darth Vader is using Han Solo as bait to draw Luke Skywalker to him prior to handing over Han to the bounty hunter.

The Trap is Laid

Darth Vader traps Han in a block of carbonite to test if it is a viable solution for capturing Luke Skywalker to take him to see the Emperor.

Luke Confronts Darth Vader

It is a clash of light sabers.

Darth Vader

Darth Vader defeats Luke in combat, and in so doing, makes a stunning revelation.

An Ambiguous Ending

Luke has survived his encounter with Darth Vader, but Han Solo has been whisked away to parts unknown, and the future is in doubt.
